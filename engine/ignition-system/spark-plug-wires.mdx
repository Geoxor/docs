---

The wires that connect the ignition coil to the [spark plugs](/engine/ignition-system/spark-plugs) on engines that feature [distributor](/engine/ignition-system/distributor) style ignition

## Identifying bad wires

Worn-out wires may bring up these issues

- Misfiring
- Sparking on valve cover engine head

<Warning>
  If a spark sound can be heard intermittently while the engine is running, it *may* be caused by the wire sparking against the engine **due to a slit** in the protective sheath
</Warning>
